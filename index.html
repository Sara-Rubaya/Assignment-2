Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

Kinder (ages 3–6) is a vital stage for development, focusing on language, social skills, motor skills, cognitive growth, and creativity. It prepares children for elementary school through play, learning, and social interaction.

Elementary School

Elementary school (ages 6–11) builds basic academic and social skills, fostering creativity and a love for learning.

Middle (10-16) Years

Middle years (10–16) are marked by academic growth, social development, physical changes, and emotional maturity, helping shape independence and identity.
